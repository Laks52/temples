Famous temples

Brihadeeswarar Temple

It is also known as Periya Kovil.Built by emperor Raja Raja Chola I and completed in 1010 AD, the temple turned 1000 years old in 2010.

Meenakshi Temple

The Meenakshi Temple in Madurai, India, is a historic Hindu temple renowned for its stunning Dravidian architecture and vibrant, intricate sculptures.

Sri Ranganatha Swamy Temple

The Sri Ranganatha Swamy Temple, located in Srirangam, Tamil Nadu, is a renowned Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ranganatha, a reclining form of Vishnu

Palani

Palani Temple, located in Tamil Nadu, India, is a renowned Hindu shrine dedicated to Lord Murugan, known for its spiritual significance and hilltop location.
